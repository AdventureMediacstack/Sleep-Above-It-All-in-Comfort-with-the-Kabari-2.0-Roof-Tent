Kabari 2.0 Features & Perks

Let’s get into the details of why the Kabari 2.0 stands above the rest:

Rapid Setup & Takedown

The gas-strut assisted clamshell mechanism means you can have your camp set up or packed away in under 60 seconds. No poles, no complicated instructions, just unclip, lift, and you’re done.

ECO13B Fabric with Light Suppression Technology (LST)

Enjoy longer sleep-ins with 23ZERO’s signature LST fabric, which blocks out sunlight and reduces internal temperatures. Plus, the ECO13B material is made from recycled content, helping reduce environmental impact.

Superior Organization

Stay organized with:

  • Ceiling Organizer (5 mesh pockets + tablet sleeve)
  • Dual Side Wall Pockets
  • New sail tracks for compatible accessories like Boot Bags, Utility Hangers, and Stash Hangers.

Panoramic Views with Superior Ventilation

The upper ventilation window with a unique rain awning allows airflow even during downpours. Multiple entry points from the sides and rear give you the flexibility to choose the best view or access point.

Integrated Pass-Through Power Dock

Stay charged with Anderson input, dual USB-A outputs, and a 12V cigarette adapter. Power your devices with ease and use the zippered pass-through slots for external battery packs or solar hookups.

Versatile Lighting

The included dimmable 24” LED light strip creates ambient interior lighting. Hook-and-loop attachments make it easy to adjust placement as needed.

Built for All Seasons

From torrential rains to desert winds, the Kabari 2.0 is engineered to handle it all. Weatherproof seals, robust hardware, and a rugged outer shell keep you protected, no matter where the trail takes you.

Comfort for Two

With its spacious sleeping area, high-density foam mattress, and condensation mat, this tent comfortably sleeps two adults without feeling cramped.

Streamlined Profile

The aerodynamic hard shell design minimizes wind resistance, improves fuel efficiency, and prevents the tent from looking like an afterthought on your rig.
